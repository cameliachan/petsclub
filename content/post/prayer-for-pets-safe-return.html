Prayer for pets safe return. For all wistful creatures in captivity that beat their wings against bars; Your sick pet can’t ask for. May we realize that as our pets trust us to take care of them, so we should trust you to take care of us,

He is like a child to us and we miss him dearly. Grant that we may trust in your mercy and send an angel to sustain us as we await their safe return. Adapted from a prayer we learned from rabbi joseph gelberman and rabbi roger ross.

We especially thank you for giving us our pets who are our friends and who bring us so much joy in life. He is my sweet, little, loving, monkey. To you they were your brothers and sisters.
